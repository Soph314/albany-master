    Appointments
    These are available during surgery hours on weekdays. A standard appointment is 15 minutes. If you have multiple problems to manage or think you will need longer please advise our receptionist.
    New patient enrolment
    We welcome new enrolments. Please drop into reception and ask for a new patient enquiry form.
    Repeat Prescriptions
    It is standard medical practice that patients are reviewed every 3 months. However if you are on regular medication for a stable medical condition your doctor may choose to renew your prescription without seeing you for 6 months. Requests for repeat prescriptions are done through reception. You will need to be specific about which medications you are requesting and allow 24 hours for this request to be followed up. There is a charge for this service. If you have not been seen in the preceding 6 months you will be asked to make an appointment with your doctor for a clinical review.
    Fees
    Payment is required on the day of consultation unless prior arrangements have been made. We accept cash, cheque, internet bill payment, EFTPOS and credit card. There is a merchant fee added if you choose to pay with credit card. Our fee schedule is displayed in the waiting room and at reception. This is also available on the Southern PHO website. Doctors are happy to discuss fees on an individual basis.
    Results
    Your doctor or nurse will notify of any abnormal results that need followed up or further investigation. For normal results we can notify you by text message. Alternately you can phone our nurse 3-5 days after blood tests were done, or 10 working days after other investigations.
    Home Visits
    When necessary these are usually scheduled during office hours. Please contact the surgery and speak to our nurse and she will discuss your needs with your doctor. Please note home visits will incur an extra fee.
